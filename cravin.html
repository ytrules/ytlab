CRAVIN.

Formed in Los Angeles in 2006, Cravin built a devoted following through relentless live performances, two full-length albums, and a string of digital singles that pushed the boundaries of modern rock.

Led by YT — producer, multi-instrumentalist, and founder of YT Creative Lab — Cravin carved their name into LA's rock scene with performances at legendary venues and international festivals.

"From Royce Hall at UCLA to the Busan International Rock Festival — Cravin played wherever the music took them."

Currently on hiatus, Cravin's catalog continues to stream worldwide. The music never stopped. Neither did we.
