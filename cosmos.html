COSMOS is a band built around something rare — women who share not just music, but a worldview. Founded in Los Angeles by Angie, COSMOS brings together Korean-American women united by their love of rock, performance, and community.

Since their debut at Whisky a Go Go on April 1, 2023, COSMOS has been steadily building their presence through local performances and festivals across the greater LA area.

"In 2025, COSMOS represented the Korean-American community as headliners at the LA Lotus Festival — a moment that defined everything they've been working toward."

Now entering their 3rd generation, Angie is seeking kindred spirits — musicians who feel music as deeply as they play it.

MILESTONES

2023

DEBUT — WHISKY A GO GO

COSMOS made their live debut at the legendary Whisky a Go Go in West Hollywood on April 1, 2023. The first generation took the stage and announced themselves to LA's rock scene.

West Hollywood · Live Debut
2023—24

LOCAL CIRCUIT & FESTIVALS

Through their 1st and 2nd generation, COSMOS built their fanbase performing at local venues and regional festivals across greater Los Angeles, growing their presence with every show.

Greater LA · Community Building
2025

LA LOTUS FESTIVAL — HEADLINER

A landmark moment: COSMOS was selected as the Korean-American representative band and headlined the LA Lotus Festival — one of LA's most celebrated cultural events.

LA Lotus Festival · Headliner · Korean-American Representative
